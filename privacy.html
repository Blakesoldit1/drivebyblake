Overview

Blake Soldit Auto Broker ("we," "us," or "Blake") operates drivebyblake.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you share with us when you visit our website or contact us about our auto brokerage services.

By using this website or submitting a pre-qualification inquiry, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name and phone number
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Vehicle preferences, budget, and payment type
  • Employment status and approximate monthly income
  • Estimated credit score range
  • Trade-in vehicle information
  • Any notes or additional details you share via our pre-qualification form or directly through text/call

We do not collect Social Security Numbers, driver's license numbers, or any hard-pull credit information through this website.

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry about vehicle sourcing and auto brokerage services
  • Help determine which vehicles and financing options may be suitable for your situation
  • Communicate with you via text, phone, or email regarding your vehicle search
  • Connect you with appropriate dealerships or lenders as part of the brokerage process

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party marketers.

Information Sharing

In the course of sourcing and negotiating your vehicle, we may share relevant details (such as your vehicle preferences and budget) with dealerships or financing partners. This is necessary to fulfill the services you've requested. We share only what is needed and only with parties directly involved in your vehicle transaction.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Pre-qualification inquiries submitted through this site are sent directly to Blake via text message and are not stored on any third-party server. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to Instagram, Facebook, and other platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those platforms and encourage you to review their individual privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
